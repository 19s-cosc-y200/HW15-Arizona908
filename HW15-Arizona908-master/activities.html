Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 15 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Kayaks are available for guest use.

Bird Watching

Bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided birdwatching trips at sunrise several times a week.
